About me

Now I am a PhD student in the Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences at McGill University starting from September 2014. I received my BSc degree in geophysics from University of Science and Technology of China in July 2014. Currently, I’m working with my supervisor Prof. Yajing Liu on induced earthquakes, crustal seismicity structures and modelling earthquake source processes.
